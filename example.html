Why Choose Goverdhan Stone Industry?

Whether you're an interior designer, an art enthusiast, or a homeowner looking to elevate your space, our stone handicrafts have the power to transform environments into showcases of elegance and cultural richness.

Conversations in Stone Goverdhan Stone has spent decades crafting Conversation in Stone. Under the direction of Ramkishan Saini, the workshop specializes in producing a broad range of objects that hark back to the exquisite craftsmanship of Rajas and Maharajas. The sculptors and artisans of Goverdhan stone, who are currently under the direction of Girraj Prasad Saini, collaborate mostly with natural sandstone from Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, and Uttar Pradesh. Sandstones come in a variety of textures and hues that are highly valued for their ability to support elaborate carvings and detailing that result in pillars, columns, fountains, panels, lamp posts, gazebos, garden ornaments, planters, pots, and human shapes. Generation after generation has continued the family's commitment to fine craftsmanship, adding to Rajasthan's diverse array of artistic expressions.

All jobs, whether they are Mother and Child series, dancers in motion, Buddha, flower pots, or deities, are unique and meticulously crafted with consideration for feel, touch, curves, and finishing.Their skill and workmanship are on display at the Vindhyachal corridor in Mirzapur, where they have built anything from residences to large-scale constructions like the Kanshi Ram Smarak, the Lucknow Lok Sabha, and the Lucknow High Court. Every sculpture communicates to the observer; the sculptor conveys a wide range of emotions, sentiments, and expressions by bringing life to stone.With years of experience, the men and women artisans work hard to create one-of-a-kind creations that perfectly capture the spirit of Rajasthani workmanship.
