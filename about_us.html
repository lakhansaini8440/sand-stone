About Us

"Goverdhan Sand Stone Industries," a reputable producer founded in 1999, specializes in sandstone curved designs as well as a range of other stone items. With decades of experience, we have established ourselves as a reliable name in the stone-working business, managing a wide range of projects for businesses, residences, hotels, and resorts. Our dedication to perfection in design, prompt delivery, and devotion to quality makes us the go-to option for customers looking for premium stone items.

Our Mission

Preservation of Craftsmanship

Our goal is to preserve the ancient methods and artistic abilities that have been handed down through the ages. Our goal is to maintain the survival and growth of these age-old skills.

Global Excellence

We aim to lead the stone handicrafts business worldwide and establish standards for excellence in design, craftsmanship, and quality.

Sustainable Practices

All of our activities are centered on sustainability, minimizing our environmental impact, and positively impacting the communities in which we operate.

Our Vision

Heritage is Celebrated

Stone artifacts remain an integral part of India's cultural identity and are celebrated across the world for their beauty and craftsmanship.

Global Search

Our products are used in homes, businesses, and public areas all over the world, adding to the appreciation of stone craftsmanship on a global scale.

Environmental Responsibility

In the sector, we set the standard for environmentally responsible and sustainable business practices.
